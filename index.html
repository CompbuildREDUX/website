What are we?

Minecraft is our way of expression; it’s our sandbox. Compbuild is a creative community founded in 2017 with a simple goal: build worlds that feel real.

We use tools like WorldEdit, VoxelSniper, and our own Compbuild Texture Pack to push creativity beyond the default game, working collaboratively on massive cityscapes, national parks, and detailed builds.
