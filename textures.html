What is Compbuild Textures?

Originally built for Compbuild’s architectural projects, the pack brings high‑fidelity materials, objects, and flora into Minecraft — perfect for creative mode, visualization, and city‑building. It’s free and continually updated.

Full‑block coverage

Hundreds of material variants and finishes suited for modern and traditional builds.

Thousands of models

Placed via custom item models & blockstates; context‑aware by biome where relevant.

Flora system

Custom trees and vegetation built for streets, parks, and courtyards.

Compatibility & requirements

Works with Minecraft: Java Edition.
Use with OptiFine or the Luminium modpack for best results.
Supports biome‑dependent blocks & custom item models.
Regular updates to track the latest versions.
Get Luminium See versions

Install in 3 steps

  1. Download the pack from Modrinth (.zip).
  2. Move it to your resourcepacks folder (Java Edition).
  3. Launch Minecraft → Options → Resource Packs → enable. For advanced features, run with OptiFine or Luminium.
