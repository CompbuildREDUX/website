What is Austin?

Austin is the capital city of Texas, located in the heart of the state along the Colorado River. Known for its vibrant live music scene, tech industry growth, and proximity to the Texas Hill Country, Austin blends southern charm with a modern, innovative spirit. It hosts major events like South by Southwest (SXSW) and the Austin City Limits (ACL) music festival, and is home to the University of Texas at Austin. The city is a hub for creativity, culture, and outdoor activities — making it one of the fastest‑growing metropolitan areas in the United States.
